The Basic Idea of Categories

The MLV Plugin will categorize the different licenses based on the configuration file into three categories:

Usually you would decide to put the licenses you will accept within your project or company into the valid category. The licenses which are not allowed into the invalid category whereas the licenses which might be investigated in detail into the warning area.
